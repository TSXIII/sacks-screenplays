So you want to write a screenplay...

Regardless of genre, every movie made in Hollywood begins the same way: as a document called a screenplay. What is a screenplay, you ask? It is a scene-by-scene breakdown of all the dialogue and action sequences a filmmaker must shoot to tell his or her story. A screenplay is often what convinces studio executives to greenlight a piece of cinema for production.

Do you have an idea for the next Oscar-winning flick? Do you want to turn that idea into something tangible that can ignite a passion for your project in the hearts and minds of others? If you find yourself in this position but are unsure where or how to start your screenwriting journey, Sack's Screenplays is here to help!

On this website, we teach the fundamentals of screenwriting. You will discover the correct way to structure a plot, as well as essential formatting tips that will make your script look more professional. To see how this material is applied to an actual screenplay, visit our "Writing Examples" page.

Sack's Screenplays is a resource from which anyone can benefit, aspiring filmmakers and curious movie buffs alike. Check us out today!
