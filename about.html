About Thomas Sack

Thomas Sack is the founder of Sack's Screenplays. He is also a filmmaker, writer, artist, and storyteller from the small town of Ellington, Connecticut. Thomas has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Film Studies from Rhode Island College, and he hopes to work professionally in Hollywood at some point in the future.
